Hummus


Description

I grew up on a diet halfway made up of hummus. It's one of my favourite foods, and I cannot emphasise how much I love hummus. This is all I can say.

You can choose whether or not to peel the chickpeas for the first step, if you really want a smoother consistency. Also, feel free to try the hummus while blending to see how chunky/seasoned you want the final product to be! Serve with either vegetables (carrots, celery, bell pepper), bread, or in a sandwich.


Ingredients

  • 15 oz can of chickpeas
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 small cloves garlic
  • 0.75 tsp salt
  • 3 ice cubes
  • Olive oil, to serve
  • Paprika, to serve
  • Pita, vegetables, or lebanese bread, to serve

Directions

  1. Optionally, peel the chickpeas within a bowl of water. The skins should float to the surface to be removed easily.
  2. Drain and dry the chickpeas thoroughly, then transfer to a food processor and pulse into a fine texture.
  3. Add the lemon juice, tahini, garlic, salt, and ice cubes. Blend until completely smooth, or to your desired texture. Adjust as needed with additional salt and lemon.
  4. Serve in a bowl with a drizzle of olive oil, sprinkle of paprika, and vegetables or bread on the side.
